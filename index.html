How to make a Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Instructions for the most scrumptious meal

Ingredients:

  • Butter
  • Kraft Mac n' Cheese
  • Egg
  • Our Family brand brownie mix
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly
  • Bread
  • Leftover meat loaf from last nights dinner
  • Iphone 6 plus

Directions:

  1. Take out three slices of bread
  2. toast the bread, spread butter of the toast to keep them warm
  3. In a large pot, boil two cups of water
  4. Once water is boiled, put Mac n' Cheese into pot
  5. Take out the powder in the brownie mix
  6. On the now melted butter, sprinkle the powder onto the toast
  7. Once the noodles are soft, strain them and put them back on the oven
  8. Add the rest of the butter into the Mac n' Cheese
  9. Spread peanut butter onto one slice of toast
  10. Spread jelly onto the other slice of toast
  11. Once butter is fully melted, insert "Cheese" into the pot
  12. Stir until creamy texture is realized
  13. With your bare hands, slather creamy Mac n' Cheese onto peanut butter and jelly sandwich
  14. Use your Iphone 6 Plus to take a picture of your food and upload it onto a social media platform of your choosing
  15. Once you have been cyberbulled enough, use the Iphone 6 Plus to order some Pizza Hut Pizza
  16. Don't order Hawaiian, that's gross
  17. Throw sandwich away and enjoy!
